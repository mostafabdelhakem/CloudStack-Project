Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What is cloud hosting?

Cloud hosting is a type of web hosting that uses multiple servers to balance load and maximize uptime. Instead of using a single server, your website draws resources from a network of connected virtual and physical servers.

How does CloudStack compare to traditional hosting?

Unlike traditional hosting that relies on a single server, CloudStack uses a network of servers, providing better reliability, scalability, and performance. This means your website can handle traffic spikes without downtime and can grow easily as your needs change.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can easily upgrade your hosting plan as your website grows. Our scalable infrastructure allows for seamless transitions between plans without any downtime or technical complications.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our hosting plans. If you're not satisfied with our services within the first month, we'll provide a full refund, no questions asked.

How do I migrate my existing website?

We offer free website migration services for all new customers. Our technical team will handle the entire process for you, ensuring a smooth transition without any data loss or downtime.

What security measures does CloudStack provide?

CloudStack provides advanced security features, including free SSL certificates, DDoS protection, daily backups, and regular security updates to keep your website safe from threats.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer 24/7 expert support via live chat, email, and phone. Our team is always ready to assist with any technical issues, ensuring your website runs smoothly at all times.

How does CloudStack ensure website performance?

CloudStack uses a global CDN, SSD storage, and optimized servers to ensure fast load times and 99.9% uptime. Our infrastructure is designed to handle high traffic with minimal latency.
